> This article explains how to co-stream the Future Games Show Summer Showcase 2024 with over 40 multi-format games including updates on Once Human, Bodycam, Detective Dotson, and Parcel Corps.


        <img alt="Cover" src="https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/6QsJyXFK4rAJgM5zUxMKYa.jpg"/>
        
We'd be thrilled if you wanted to share over 40 new trailers and reveals from the upcoming Future Games Show Summer Showcase on June 8 with your audience, whether you're an established streamer, or starting to build a community.

Our Summer Showcase will be hosted on Twitch, [YouTube,](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1jXmyz452hs) Twitter, and all leading social networks including TikTok. We'll be broadcasting the live show in English, with a live selection of translations for French, Spanish, German, Japanese, Simplified Chinese, Korean, ASL and BSL.

Anyone can stream the show. See our easy-to-follow instructions below.

With over 40 multi-format games on PS5, Xbox, PC, Switch, and mobile platforms; nine world premieres, fresh demo drops, plus the return of our exciting post show, the FGS Expansion Pack, this will be our most-diverse, creative, show yet with loads of great new titles to share with your audience, including updates on Once Human, Bodycam, Detective Dotson, Parcel Corps and more.



In this article, we'll go into detail about how you can co-stream the Future Games Show Summer Showcase 2024.



Co-streaming the Future Games Show is simple. All you need to do is add a new Browser Source. To do this in OBS (Open Broadcaster Software), head to the Sources panel, hover your mouse over Add, and select Browser from the menu. On XSplit, you can add a new Webpage source, then put in our stream URL:

[YouTube](https://www.gamesradar.com/tag/youtube/) stream ([https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjHuhDqc0Wc](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1jXmyz452hs))
Twitch stream ([<u>https://www.twitch.tv/futuregamesshow</u>](https://www.twitch.tv/futuregamesshow) & [<u>https://www.twitch.tv/gamesradar</u>](https://www.twitch.tv/gamesradar))

After that, just arrange the screen how you want it, add the co-stream tag #FutureGamesShowCostream if you're streaming via Twitch, and then it's time to bring the Future Games Show Summer Showcase on June 8, 2024 to your audience.Â 

Having problems with your co-stream? For more detailed guidance, check out [Twitch's official content-sharing guidelines](https://help.twitch.tv/s/article/twitch-content-sharing-guidelines?language=en_US#co-streaming) with step-by-step instructions.

We're working with more exciting partners with each show thanks to your support. No matter the size of your audience, we'd love for you to be able to share the show with them, along with your thoughts on the event. Don't forget to tag your streams with the #FutureGamesShow hashtag if you're sharing your stream on Twitter.
