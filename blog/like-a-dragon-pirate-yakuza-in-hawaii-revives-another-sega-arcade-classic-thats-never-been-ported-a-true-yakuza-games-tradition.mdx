> Like A Dragon: Pirate Yakuza in Hawaii continues the series' tradition of resurrecting Sega arcade classics,  featuring a playable 1998 Ocean Hunter cabinet recreation alongside returning mini-games like burger delivery and karaoke.


        <img alt="Cover" src="https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/apNKTfPckvV63GwpeMryUF.jpg"/>
        
Yakuza games have a knack for resurrecting oft-forgotten classics from [Sega](https://www.gamesradar.com/tag/sega/)'s arcade cabinet era, and the upcoming, delightfully-named [Like A Dragon: Pirate Yakuza in Hawaii](https://www.gamesradar.com/like-a-dragon-pirate-yakuza-in-hawaii/) is continuing the series tradition.

Developer RGG Studios just released a [blog post](https://ryu-ga-gotoku.com/pirate/adventure/) diving deep into all the whacky side activities featured in the latest Yakuza kind-of-spin-off. Chief among them is a arcade game called The Ocean Hunter, which has never been ported off of its cabinet origins since it came out in 1998. It's almost the perfect choice for Pirate Yakuza in Hawaii because, as you can probably guess from its name, the game is all about hunting marine predators like sharks and other underwater monsters.Â

"The arcade games that can be played in the Like A Dragon series require the 'reproduction of the cabinet,'" technical lead Yutaka Ito wrote in a recent tweet. "This is also a very difficult task, but with the help of many people, we were able to reproduce the Ocean Hunter cabinet in great detail." You can see the entire cabinet, recreated in painstaking detail, in the tweet above.

Yakuza and Like A Dragon have had a long history of excavating old Sega arcade games, recreating the cabinets, and making the entire things playable within the Yakuza series itself - everything from Daytona USA 2 to Virtua Fighter 2 were ported inside of Yakuza games. And, hey, maybe some of that effort is what led [RGG to return to Virtua Fighter with the newly-revealed reboot](https://www.gamesradar.com/games/fighting/sega-is-finally-reviving-the-worlds-first-3d-fighting-game-franchise-after-leaving-it-in-arcade-purgatory-for-18-years/).

Like A Dragon: Pirate Yakuza in Hawaii is also bringing back some other returning favorite mini-games, too, like the burger delivery frenzy from Infinite Wealth, a dating mini-game, and, of course, karaoke, because it wouldn't be a Yakuza game without karaoke.
